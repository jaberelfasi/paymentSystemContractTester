Contract between Add Media (Group) Ltd & Durcan Fencing
Thank you for choosing Add People. This contract document briefly covers what we will do and what it will cost.
Summary of Your Service
We will build you a new Google AdWords campaign to channel your paid search traffic and offer a helpdesk service to provide ongoing support and reporting on your campaign.  
What We Will Do For You
For your PPC campaign build we will:
•	Conduct keyword research and analysis
•	Create your brand new Google AdWords account (if required)
•	Build your brand new Google AdWords campaign
•	Segment your keywords into targeted Ad Groups
•	Compose ad copy to attract visitors, tailored by Ad Group
•	Launch your AdWords campaign
•	Install and link Google Analytics to your Google Adwords account (if required)
Your Ongoing Helpdesk Support
You will have access to our team of PPC experts, who will provide the following services:
•	Conduct a monthly review of your AdWords campaign
•	Make changes to your AdWords campaign to improve performance
•	Provide monthly performance reporting
•	Suggest strategies and additional products that could help your business
Our Responsibilities
Our team of experts will undertake work on your Google AdWords campaign based on any previous results available and the information you provide us about your business, however we are not responsible for the performance of your website which will affect the number of conversions and sales we achieve.
